1. Scatter Plot

Energy Consumption vs Energy Star Rating

This chart explores the relationship between the energy efficiency rating of televisions and their annual energy consumption.
Each point represents a television model. The visualisation helps identify whether higher star-rated TVs consume less electricity.

2. Donut Chart

Energy Consumption by Screen Technology

This chart aggregates the average energy consumption across different television screen technologies (e.g., LCD, LED, OLED).
It provides an overview of how energy usage varies between technologies.

3. Bar Chart

Energy Consumption for 55-inch TVs

This chart compares the average yearly energy consumption of 55-inch televisions across different screen technologies.
By focusing on a single size category, the comparison between technologies becomes clearer.

4. Line Chart

Electricity Spot Prices (1998–2024)

This chart visualises the trend of electricity spot prices over time.
It highlights how electricity costs have changed historically and allows users to observe long-term trends in the dataset.
