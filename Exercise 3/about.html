About PowerWise

Our Mission

PowerWise aims to help Australian households understand and reduce energy consumption from common appliances — especially home entertainment products like televisions.

We provide clear, practical information based on Australian Government energy rating data and real-world usage patterns to support better purchasing decisions and more sustainable habits.

Why Focus on Energy Efficiency?

With rising electricity prices and growing environmental awareness, small changes in appliance choice and usage can lead to meaningful savings — both financial and environmental.
