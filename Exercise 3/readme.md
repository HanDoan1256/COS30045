Key point: Assuming the audiences are non-technical household decision-makers, who are mostly interested in cost savings, energy effienciency and practical actions only, the charts displayed must be simple and deliver key neccessary messages. 

Data Story:
Audience:
This data story is designed for household decision-makers who are interested in purchasing a television while keeping long-term energy costs low.

Audience Interests:
The audience is primarily concerned with understanding how different types of televisions compare in terms of energy consumption and whether choosing a larger screen size results in significantly higher electricity usage.

The two questions chosen to answer their needs will be:
1. Which screen technology is the most recomended (has the highest star rating)?
2. What brand's TV is the most energy-efficiency?

Key Insights:
1. Certain screen technologies achieve higher average star ratings, making them the most recommended options based on overall rating.
2. Some TV brands consume less power on average, indicating better energy efficiency compared to others.
